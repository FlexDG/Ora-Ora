Our Story

Living well begins
with eating well.

Although our shop may have limited tables and chairs, we're much more than just a tapsilogan and bigger than you think. We're the place where friends and family come together and a comforting escape for giving yourself a “me time” while munching on some of your favorite local silog meals.

Most Popular Item

Tapsilog
with Greens

Experience the essence of Filipino culinary delight at Ora-Ora in Angeles City. Our signature dish, Tapsilog with Greens, is a true masterpiece. Savor the perfect harmony of savory marinated beef, garlic-fried rice, and a perfectly fried egg, all topped with a medley of fresh, vibrant greens. Each bite encapsulates the rich flavors and warm hospitality of the Philippines, making it a must-try when you dine with us. Explore our website and discover the heart and soul of Filipino cuisine, beautifully crafted in our Tapsilog with Greens. It's more than a dish; it's a cultural journey.
